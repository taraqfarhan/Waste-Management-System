Overview

Secondary Transfer
Stations

Rajshahi City Corporation (RCC) has established multiple modern Secondary Transfer Stations (STSs) to manage daily municipal waste (~420 tons) hygienically. These stations act as intermediate storage points to efficiently transfer waste to the final landfill, reducing environmental pollution in residential areas.

Modernization Initiative RCC plans to construct 30 STSs in phases to upgrade waste management from traditional open dumping.
Key Locations Terakhadia, Kazla, Sapura, Paba, Seroil Railway Station, Kashiadanga, Padma, and Shalbagan.
Functionality Waste is collected, temporarily stored, then transferred efficiently — minimising disposal in public spaces.
Recent Developments New stations opened to meet growing city demands, with ongoing operational improvements citywide.
